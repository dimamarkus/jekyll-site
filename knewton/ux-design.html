UX Design

Business process maps and user journeys help to better understand a user's experience and design it from a higher level.

Low fidelity wireframe timelines can reveal application components for developers as well as serve as a baseline for visual design.

Complicated content leads to many iterations of a particular UI module exploring various data hierarchies and interaction models.
